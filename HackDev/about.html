HackDev is a platform where students learn something more; something more interesting than just coding and regular coursework. This wing introduces: Hack- Cybersecurity and Ethical Hacking and Dev- Web Development, Android Development, Game Development and one more crucial aspect, that is, Data Science.
The main objective of HackDev Community is to foster an interest in different aspects of Information Technology, outside the classroom and introduce students to the wonders and relevance of computer science in our lives.
'HackDev Community' includes activities such as distribution of e-books, trusted articles on the required topics, guidance from people working in the industry. Moreover, notes, lectures and projects has been made available to students for learning.
